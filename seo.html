What is Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

Before moving towards the concept of SEO marketing, we must understand the concept and basic definition of the search engine optimization process. Search Engine Optimization is abbreviated as SEO. And in general, the abbreviated form SEO is the most commonly used by others. Search Engine Optimization is the organic method of bringing the quality and high quantity of internet users to a particular website/e-commerce store. This technique helps the website/e-commerce stores to achieve the increased quantity and quality of internet traffic and well-oriented brand exposure to the potential client in the digital market. As the name suggests, SEO is very much related to the search engines like Google, Bing, Yahoo, etc. SEO helps your website to get easily searched on these search engines.

SEO is more about reading the internet users’ minds and predicting their online search habits. This technique accurately suggests the intent of people searching something online with a set of keywords or queries. They query or keyword what users are searching for, like the question they have asked and the search results they are getting from the Search engines are the answers from the various websites. In simple words, SEO makes your websites stand in the questionnaire on the search engines.

Types of Search Engine Optimization Service

SEO can be divided on the basis of the method followed and the area targeted. On the basis of the method implemented for the process of SEO, it is of two types; White Hat and Black Hat. Black Hat SEO is mostly considered invalid by Google and almost banned due to malicious activities. However, all Black Hat SEO was not involved in malicious activities but due to some fraudulent activities, search engines have demotivated this mode of SEO. White Hat SEO is the most accepted process of SEO. We only follow the white hat technique for doing the SEO. On the basis of the targeted area, SEO can also be divided into two categories; Local SEO services and National SEO services. There are two types of SEO practices that are followed by Fausta technologies. Those two types of SEO services are:

Local SEO Services: In this practice of SEO, we aim to boost the sale of a local service provider like local restaurants, juice corners, laundry service, health service, etc. This practice helps the local service providers to get more locally based customers by improving the local search results.

National SEO Services: This practice is similar to Local SEO services in terms of the ranking of broad keywords. But it may differ in the range of geographical areas, and it is a long-term investment that helps businesses to compete with each other.
