Do’s while writing SEO-focused content​

Writing SEO-focused content is quite important nowadays. As it will not only help the SEO team but will also help in avoiding rewriting the content as per the technical standards. One must need to follow below mentioned guidelines while writing content focused on search engine optimization.

Pick your Keyword before writing​

Before starting the writing, the writer must do their keyword research properly. Pick one main keyword and three to four other keywords that need to be inserted into the content. Picking keywords before writing the content will help you to avoid the re-writing or post-insertion of keywords in the articles or blogs.

Research your topic properly​

Yes, you have to decide and take care of your target audience while writing the content. You can’t expect everyone to read your article or blogs. The reading habits of each and every person is different. Your topic of writing might be interested in a specific group of people. But it can be boring or of no use for another group of people. Don’t be in a rush to impress everyone. It is always suggested to define and write the content according to your target audience.

Try to answer the query of the audience​

The content writers need to focus more on giving solutions to the query to their audiences. While writing the content one must focus on the possible question that can be raised. And it will be best if you write the whole content in the questionnaire format. It will attract more readers to your article.

Insert a call to action in your article​

Your article must be problem-solving in nature. And if you are solving a particular problem through your content, then it must include any call to action. The call to action is a tactic to keep your reader engaged with your website and articles. This call to action may include subscribing newsletter, call experts, free downloads, etc.

What to avoid while content writing?​

This is the most important section for content writers and SEO managers. As if one cannot follow important “dos” in their content, then at least they can try to avoid “don’ts” while writing.

Avoid giving suggestion to readers​

A content writer is able to write about almost every topic in the universe. But this ability doesn’t make them expert about each and every topic of the universe. And, if you are not an expert in a particular topic you can’t suggest it personally to your readers. You can provide references but not suggestions.

Don’t rush behind the high volume of keywords​

The high influx of keywords doesn’t guarantee more search volume. Yes but forcefully increase of keywords may destroy the structure of your content.

Don’t go for the exact number of keywords​

There is no specific number of keywords to be inserted in a particular content. Any number of keywords can be magical. Even the SEO tools say that this number is recommended. They don’t provide a guarantee for that exact number. Don’t disturb your writing structure while trying to keep the number of a particular keyword in control.

Stop following the keywords tool blindly​

Yes, you heard that right!!! Stops follow the keyword tools blindly. These keywords tools don’t provide the latest data about trendy keywords. For example, many keywords fail to recognize the importance of the word “COVID-19” or “Alzheimer’s Disease” but in actual they are the most searched words in the world.
